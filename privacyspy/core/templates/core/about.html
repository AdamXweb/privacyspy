About

We believe that users have the right to know exactly how companies store, process, and share their personal data.

Privacy policies satisfy this right but they are inaccessible. PrivacySpy aims to change this by creating a look-up platform containing a standardized and consistent rubric that serves as a proxy of privacy policies' quality.
